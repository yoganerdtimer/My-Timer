🧘 Yoga Timer - Help & User Guide

App version 1.20

Welcome to Yoga Timer

Yoga Timer is a simple and elegant app designed to help you maintain a consistent yoga practice by timing your poses and transitions. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced practitioner, this app will help you stay focused on your practice.

Getting Started

Creating Your First Timer

  1. Tap the + button in the top right corner of the "My Yoga Timers" section
  2. Enter a name for your timer (e.g., "Morning Flow", "Evening Stretch")
  3. Set the number of poses (1-100)
  4. Set the interval between poses in seconds (3 seconds to 20 minutes)
  5. Tap Save to create your timer
💡 Tip: Start with shorter intervals (15-30 seconds) if you're new to yoga, and gradually increase as you become more comfortable.

Using a Timer

Starting a Session

  1. Find your timer in the list
  2. Tap the Start button
  3. The timer will begin immediately with a bell sound
  4. Move to the next pose when you hear the bell

During a Session

Active Timer Display: When a timer is running, you'll see a detailed display at the top of the screen showing:

  • Timer Name: The name of your active timer
  • Time Remaining: Total time left in the entire session
  • Current Pose Time: Time remaining for the current pose with progress bar
  • Pose Progress: Current pose number and overall session progress
  • Controls: Pause, Resume, and Stop buttons

Pausing and Resuming

  • Tap Pause to temporarily stop the timer
  • Tap Resume to continue from where you left off
  • The timer state is saved automatically, so you can even close the app and resume later

Stopping a Session

  • Tap Stop to end the current session
  • All timer data will be cleared
  • You can start a new session anytime

Managing Timers

Editing a Timer

  1. Find the timer you want to edit in the list
  2. Tap the Edit icon (pencil icon)
  3. Modify the name, number of poses, or interval
  4. Tap Save to update
⚠️ Note: If you edit a timer that's currently running, the timer will restart with the new settings.

Deleting a Timer

  1. Find the timer you want to delete
  2. Tap the Delete icon (trash icon)
  3. Confirm the deletion

Settings

Accessing Settings

Tap the gear icon (⚙️) in the top right corner of the main screen to access settings.

Available Settings

  • Sound: Enable or disable bell sounds for pose transitions
  • Dark Mode: Switch between light and dark theme
💡 Tip: The bell sound helps you stay focused without looking at your device. Keep it enabled for the best experience!

Understanding the Timer Display

Overall Progress

The main timer shows your overall session progress, including:

  • Total time remaining for the entire session
  • Overall completion percentage
  • Current pose number out of total poses

Current Pose Progress

The current pose timer shows:

  • Time remaining for the current pose
  • Progress bar indicating how far through the current pose you are
  • Percentage of the current pose completed

Example: If you have a 10-pose timer with 30-second intervals:

  • Total session time: 5 minutes (10 × 30 seconds)
  • Each pose lasts 30 seconds
  • Bell rings at the start of each new pose

Tips for Best Experience

  • Prepare your space: Set up your yoga mat and any props before starting
  • Keep device nearby: Place your device where you can hear the bell but won't be distracted
  • Use headphones: For better audio clarity, especially in noisy environments
  • Practice regularly: Consistency is key to building a yoga habit
  • Adjust intervals: Start with shorter intervals and increase as you progress
  • Focus on breathing: Use the bell as a reminder to check your breath and alignment

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use the app in the background?

A: Yes! The timer continues running even if you switch to another app or lock your device. The bell sound will still play at each transition.

Q: What happens if I close the app during a session?

A: Your timer state is automatically saved. When you reopen the app, you can resume your session from where you left off (if it was running when you closed it).

Q: Can I have multiple timers?

A: Yes! You can create as many timers as you like for different types of practices (morning flow, evening stretch, etc.).

Q: Why don't I hear the bell sound?

A: Check your device's volume settings and make sure "Sound" is enabled in the app settings. Also, ensure your device is not in silent or Do Not Disturb mode.

Q: Can I edit a timer while it's running?

A: Yes, but the timer will restart with the new settings. It's best to edit timers when they're not active.

Q: Does the app work offline?

A: Yes! The app works completely offline. No internet connection is required.
