🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: · App version 1.30

Introduction

Welcome to Yoga Timer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Key Principle: Yoga Timer is designed with privacy in mind. Your yoga timers and settings are stored locally on your device. Optional features—full-screen ads via Google AdMob and in-app purchases via Google Play Billing—use Google’s services and may involve network access and identifiers as described below.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Yoga Timer stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Yoga Timer Configurations: Names, number of poses, and intervals for timers you create
  • App Settings: Your preferences for sound, dark mode, and other app settings
  • Active Timer State: Current timer progress, pose number, and time remaining (only while a timer is active)

Important: All this data is stored locally on your device using Android's built-in storage mechanisms (Room Database and SharedPreferences). We do not have access to this data, and it never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it or back up your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect (on our own)

We do not run our own servers for Yoga Timer and we do not ask you to create an account in the app. We do not collect your name, email, or phone number for our own use.

  • Location: We do not collect GPS or precise location.
  • Our analytics: We do not add separate analytics SDKs beyond what Google provides through ads and the Play Store as described below.

When ads or purchases are used: Google AdMob and Google Play may process device and advertising-related data according to Google’s Privacy Policy. That can include use of the advertising identifier for advertising and related analytics, subject to your device and Google account settings.

How We Use Your Information

Local Data Usage

The data stored on your device is used solely for:

  • Storing and managing your yoga timer configurations
  • Maintaining your app preferences (sound, dark mode, etc.)
  • Preserving timer state when you pause or temporarily close the app
  • Providing the core functionality of the app

What we don’t do with your local timer data

We do not sell your yoga timer configurations, and we do not upload them to servers we operate. We do not use your locally stored timers for marketing.

Google’s services (AdMob, Play Billing, Play distribution) are subject to Google’s policies. When you view an ad or complete a purchase, Google may process data as described in their documentation and privacy policy.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Room Database: For storing your yoga timer configurations
  • SharedPreferences: For storing app settings (sound, theme), data used for the 48-hour ad schedule (e.g. first-launch time and last time an App Open ad was shown), and a local flag for whether you purchased Remove ads (kept in sync with Google Play when possible)

Security Measures

  • Your timer and settings data is stored in your device’s private app storage, accessible only by Yoga Timer
  • Internet access may be used when you load ads (AdMob), complete purchases (Google Play), or open links (e.g. help) in a browser—those flows are handled by Google or your browser, not by servers we operate

Your Control: You have complete control over your data. You can delete individual timers in the app or clear all app data through Android Settings → Apps → Yoga Timer → Storage → Clear Data.

Permissions

Yoga Timer does not request sensitive permissions (location, camera, microphone, contacts, etc.). Your timer data stays on your device. The app may use the network connection for optional advertising (see below), in-app purchases processed by Google Play, and opening help links in a browser.

Most app data is stored in the app's private storage, which Android grants automatically.

Advertising & purchases

Advertising identifier & compliance

Advertising identifier: Where applicable, use of the advertising identifier (and related ad technologies) is limited to advertising and, where supported by the ad platform, user analytics tied to those services. We respect your choices, including Android and Google ad personalization and opt-out settings. We do not combine the advertising identifier with personally identifiable information unless we obtain appropriate consent where required. This policy describes our practices clearly; see also Google’s policies linked below.

App Open ads (AdMob) and 48-hour frequency

Yoga Timer may show App Open (full-screen) advertisements served by Google AdMob when your device is online. App Open ads may appear when you launch or return to the app in line with our implementation and Google’s formats.

Frequency: We use a 48-hour window:

  • The first time you may see an ad is no earlier than 48 hours after the app records your first launch on this installation.
  • After an ad has been displayed, the next eligible ad is no earlier than 48 hours later (measured from when the ad was shown).

If you purchase Remove ads, we do not request App Open ads for your install. AdMob may use identifiers and device data as described in Google’s Privacy Policy. You can limit ad personalization in your Google account or device settings.

One-time in-app purchase (Remove ads)

Remove ads is an optional one-time (non-subscription) purchase processed by Google Play Billing. Google handles payment and receipt validation; we do not receive your full card details. Purchase state may be stored locally on your device and synchronized with Google Play when you use Restore purchase or reinstall on a device signed into the same Google account.

Product availability and pricing are set in Google Play Console; you should install the app from the Google Play Store for purchases to work as intended.

Third-Party Services

Besides AdMob and Google Play (billing and app distribution), Yoga Timer does not integrate additional analytics or social SDKs. Core timer features work without an account; only ads and purchases require Google’s services when you use those features.

Children's Privacy

Yoga Timer’s core features (local timers) do not require an account. If the app shows ads, Google’s ad services may apply age-related and personalization rules per Google’s policies and your device settings. Parents and guardians can use device and Google account controls for ads, and may use Remove ads (one-time purchase) to avoid ad content in the app.

Data Deletion

How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your data in the following ways:

  • Delete Individual Timers: Use the delete button in the app to remove specific timers
  • Clear App Data: Go to Android Settings → Apps → Yoga Timer → Storage → Clear Data to remove all timers and settings

Note: Clearing app data will permanently remove all your timers and settings. This action cannot be undone.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and the "Last Updated" date will be modified. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify users through the app or app store listing.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: You can view all your data directly in the app
  • Modify: You can edit or delete timers and change settings at any time
  • Delete: You can delete all data by clearing app data in Android settings (Settings → Apps → Yoga Timer → Storage → Clear Data)
  • Portability: Your data is stored in standard Android formats and can be accessed through device backups
