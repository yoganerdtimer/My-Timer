Introduction

Welcome to Yoga Timer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Key Principle: Yoga Timer is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary for the app to function, and all data is stored locally on your device.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Yoga Timer stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Yoga Timer Configurations: Names, number of poses, and intervals for timers you create
  • App Settings: Your preferences for sound, dark mode, and other app settings
  • Active Timer State: Current timer progress, pose number, and time remaining (only while a timer is active)

Important: All this data is stored locally on your device using Android's built-in storage mechanisms (Room Database and SharedPreferences). We do not have access to this data, and it never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it or back up your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports (unless explicitly enabled)
  • Any data transmitted over the internet

How We Use Your Information

Local Data Usage

The data stored on your device is used solely for:

  • Storing and managing your yoga timer configurations
  • Maintaining your app preferences (sound, dark mode, etc.)
  • Preserving timer state when you pause or temporarily close the app
  • Providing the core functionality of the app

No Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Share your data with third parties
  • Sell your data to advertisers or data brokers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Transmit your data over the internet

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Room Database: For storing your yoga timer configurations
  • SharedPreferences: For storing app settings and preferences

Security Measures

  • Data is stored in your device's private app storage, accessible only by Yoga Timer
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No external servers or cloud services are used
  • Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

Your Control: You have complete control over your data. You can delete individual timers, clear all app data through Android settings, or uninstall the app to remove all data.

Permissions

Yoga Timer does not request any special permissions. The app does not use vibration, notifications, internet access, location, camera, microphone, contacts, or any storage outside its own private app data.

All data is stored in the app's private storage, which Android grants automatically. No runtime permission prompts are shown to the user.

Third-Party Services

Yoga Timer does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely offline and does not integrate with any external services that would collect or transmit your data.

Children's Privacy

Yoga Timer is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect personal information or transmit data over the internet, there are no special privacy concerns for children. The app can be used by anyone without privacy risks.

Data Deletion

How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your data in several ways:

  • Delete Individual Timers: Use the delete button in the app to remove specific timers
  • Clear App Data: Go to Android Settings → Apps → Yoga Timer → Storage → Clear Data
  • Uninstall the App: Uninstalling the app will remove all associated data from your device

Note: Since all data is stored locally, deleting the app or clearing app data will permanently remove all your timers and settings. This action cannot be undone.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and the "Last Updated" date will be modified. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify users through the app or app store listing.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: You can view all your data directly in the app
  • Modify: You can edit or delete timers and change settings at any time
  • Delete: You can delete all data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app
  • Portability: Your data is stored in standard Android formats and can be accessed through device backups

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through:

  • The app store listing (for reviews and feedback)
  • Email support (if provided in app store listing)

Our Commitment: We are committed to maintaining your privacy and will respond to any privacy-related inquiries promptly and transparently.

Consent

By using Yoga Timer, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, you may stop using the app and delete it from your device.
